Techniques

These techniques create a volume effect without damaging the health of your natural lashes. Technological fan, flat and lightweight shape.

Classic Eyelash

The application consists of 1x1, where one extension is applied to each natural lash. The thickness and length are selected appropriately to avoid affecting the natural lash follicle.

From $95

Hawaiian

Double-tipped lash that creates the effect of greater density and volume in the eye. The application is also 1x1, the only difference is the shape of the lash, which simulates a "Y".

From $110

4D Volumen

This technique involves applying four ultra-light extensions to each natural lash, creating a fuller yet natural look. Ideal for those seeking a noticeable but soft volume enhancement without feeling too dramatic.

From $120

5D Volumen

Five fine extensions are fanned and applied to each natural lash, giving the eyes a bold, fluffy, and more glamorous appearance. Perfect for clients who want a richer volume while maintaining elegance.

From $130

6D Volumen

Six ultra-light lashes are applied to each natural lash, delivering maximum density and drama. This is the ultimate volume technique for those who love a bold, eye-catching look without compromising lash health.

From $140
